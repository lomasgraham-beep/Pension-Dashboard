Members (members build: crud2)

Connecting to your database…

These are the people the model is built around. Name identifies the member everywhere (person 1 / person 2 are derived alphabetically). Date of birth drives the ages used in the drawdown projection, and end-of-life date sets how many years the projection runs. Each member is saved separately, and nothing is stored until you tap that member's Save. Birth year is kept in sync with the date of birth automatically. After saving, refresh the dashboard to pick up the change.
