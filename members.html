Connecting to your database…

These are the people the model is built around. Date of birth drives the ages used in the drawdown projection, and end-of-life date sets how many years the projection runs. Each member is saved separately, and nothing is stored until you tap that member's Save. Birth year is kept in sync with the date of birth automatically, so the two can't drift apart. After saving, refresh the dashboard or Scenario Lab to pick up the change.
