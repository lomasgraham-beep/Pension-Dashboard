Pensions (pensions build: wp-flag)

Connecting to your database…

The pension pots the model tracks, grouped by owner. You can edit the name, type and owner of each. Each pot is saved separately and nothing is stored until you tap its Save; Reload discards edits.

One important note on type: only pots set to Defined Contributions appear in the dashboard's Log Entry form (those are the ones you log balances for). The type is a dropdown to keep that value exact.
