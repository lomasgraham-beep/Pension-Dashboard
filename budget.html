Monthly Budget

Per-person budget for the month you're viewing: net income in, the bills that actually leave each account that month, and what's left. Use the controls to step to next month or jump ahead; the muted avg figure is the smoothed 12-month monthly cost. PayPal shows as one line in the account it draws from; pensions are gathered into their own block and taken off once at the end. Savings set-asides, income and pension contributions stay at today's level. The Credit Card box is an estimate for reference only and is not part of any total here. Insurances due in the month shown are listed under Cash Summary. (budget build: v23)
