Fuel

Used to cost each holiday's driving from its round-trip miles.

Fuel per holiday = round-trip miles ÷ MPG = gallons, × 4.546 = litres, × price per litre. Towing uses the towing MPG.

Holiday savings pot

The savings account that holds the money set aside for holidays. The monthly set-aside (annual holiday cost ÷ 12) shows on the Budget against the account that funds this pot — the same way insurance savings work. Pots are accounts with “Funded from” set on Bank Accounts.
