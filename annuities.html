Annuities (build: ann3)

Model buying an annuity during retirement. At the purchase date the purchase amount is taken from that member’s DC pot (drawn proportionally across tax-free and taxable) and converted into a lifelong income of amount × annuity rate. That income joins the member’s guaranteed income and pays towards the bills before their remaining pot is drawn down. Escalation 0 = a level (fixed) annuity. If the pot is too small at the purchase month the annuity is skipped. You can add more than one.
