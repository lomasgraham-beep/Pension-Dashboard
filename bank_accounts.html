Bank Accounts

Accounts used to pay bills and pension contributions. For security, the account ID is a short alphanumeric label of your choosing (e.g. "xxxx651") — not the full account number. Set "Funded from" when an account is a savings pot topped up from another account (e.g. money put aside for insurance and car bills) — the Budget then attributes that spending to the funding account.
